About Me

Detailed oriented person that thrives on handling many tasks. Let’s keep all the balls moving to completion. I am a cooperative team player who is always focused on the goal. I am flexible and easy going, most things don’t rattle me. I like to think outside the box. I will find a different way to use a tool than it was made for.

It confuses some people, excites others. I like making charts, lists and word documents to see a progression of getting things done. I am creative with a touch of independence. Give me a chance to be inventive and I will surprise you.
